Our History



             We are BMW

As the BMW company, we are proud of our long and successful history. Our story began in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, and since then, we have become one of the world's most respected and admired automakers. Our commitment to innovation and quality is at the heart of everything we do. From our first successful product, the BMW IIIa aircraft engine, to our latest electric vehicles, we have always been at the forefront of technological advancement and engineering excellence. We are proud to have won numerous awards and accolades for our vehicles, including multiple "Car of the Year" titles, and to be consistently ranked among the top luxury automakers in the world. But our success is not just about our vehicles. We are also committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact. We have set ambitious goals for reducing our carbon footprint and increasing the use of renewable energy in our production processes. We also value diversity and inclusion, and strive to create a work environment that welcomes and respects people from all backgrounds. As a company, we are always looking to the future with excitement and optimism. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of what is possible, and to continue making innovative and high-quality vehicles that bring joy and excitement to our customers.We are proud to be a part of the BMW family, and we look forward to many more years of success and innovation.
